# 🌐 Future Roadmap: Civic Intelligence Dashboard (`civicforensics.org`)

This repository serves as the practical foundation for the upcoming **Civic Intelligence Dashboard** — a planned AI-assisted platform designed to monitor public narratives, institutional decisions and governance risks through structured data analysis and Civic Forensics methodology.

## 🎯 Platform objectives

- **Visual insights:** transforming administrative obfuscation into clean dashboard indicators.
- **Procedural inconsistencies:** automatically flagging when public institutional statements contradict legal outputs.
- **Democratic resilience indicators:** measuring patterns of transparency, opacity and institutional avoidance across regional ecosystems.
- **Public-interest traceability:** connecting each conclusion to a verifiable document, date, legal basis, signature, deadline or metadata trace.

## 🧩 Proposed modules

1. **Document intake layer** — collection of administrative acts, FOI responses, public statements, minutes, permits, decisions and metadata.
2. **Privacy and anonymization layer** — removal of sensitive personal data before public use or AI processing.
3. **Prompt analysis layer** — quick triage, deep legal analysis, FOI generation, appeal drafting and narrative auditing.
4. **Evidence normalization layer** — conversion of findings into structured fields: institution, act, date, signatory, legal basis, issue, proof, risk and recommended action.
5. **Risk scoring layer** — scoring of formalism, silence of administration, contradiction, missing reasoning and public-interest risk.
6. **Dashboard layer** — civic intelligence cards, timelines, maps, contradiction matrices and accountability indicators.

## 🧪 MVP hypothesis

A minimal working prototype should prove one core claim: a citizen or researcher can take one administrative document and, through a repeatable workflow, transform it into a structured, evidence-based accountability record.

The first MVP should support:

- manual document input,
- privacy checklist,
- quick triage prompt,
- deep analysis prompt,
- FOI or appeal draft generation,
- JSON export,
- simple dashboard card.
